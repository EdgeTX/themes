# Outdoor_Clean — EdgeTX Theme

A no-frills theme built for pilots who need to read their radio at a glance —
not admire it. Designed around RC glider flying, where a quick scan of
telemetry and switch states matters more than visual polish.

# Design philosophy
Clean, uncluttered, and readable. The light gray-white background
(#F0F2F0) reduces glare in direct sunlight while keeping text sharp.
All color choices prioritize legibility over aesthetics: a cool blue family
carries the UI structure, a warm orange marks active states, and a strong
red flags anything that needs immediate attention.
The theme deliberately avoids busy backgrounds, decorative elements, and
low-contrast color combinations. Every color earns its place by making
information faster to read.

# Compatible radios
This theme includes background images for all EdgeTX color screen
resolutions.

# Installation

Copy the Outdoor_Clean folder to THEMES/ on your SD card.
On your radio:

System Settings -> Theme → select Outdoor_Clean

The correct background image for your screen size is picked
automatically by EdgeTX.

The theme requires EdgeTX 2.9 or later. For QM_BG and QM_FG support
(quick-menu colors), EdgeTX 2.12 or later is recommended.
